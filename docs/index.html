Welcome!

What were you expecting? Maybe advertisements, some loading circle gimmick that took up the whole page, or maybe a 3 second wait before it even loaded? Unfortunately, If you're a fan of those things you've come to the wrong place. Why is this the wrong place you ask? Well, because this site is static! Unlike a traditional dynamic website where content is generated, you guessed it, dynamically upon loading the page, this site has been pre-rendered for your viewing pleasure. If you're wondering why that's important, you can see the benefits already. This site is

  • Fast
  • Responsive
  • Light on server resources

And on top of all that, it looks great thanks to its easy templating system. Feel free to check out the other pages and take note of these benefits.
